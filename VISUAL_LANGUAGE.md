# PAGMAR - Visual Language

## Background
- Deep black / dark blue (#010508 to #030308)
- Subtle vignette (darker at edges)
- Faint ambient particle field at all times

## Point Reveal Stages (CORE VISUAL LOGIC)
Each point has reveal stages that form ONE CONTINUOUS METAPHOR:
"The light breaks, and meaning begins to grow."

The prism and the fern are not separate decorations.
- The PRISM is the mechanism of interpretation: light splits into possibilities.
- The FERN/ROOT is the form of revelation: meaning grows slowly from a point.
Together they are one event.

### 1. DORMANT
- Tiny faint dot, opacity 5-10%. Almost invisible. Just a presence.

### 2. AWAKENING
- Soft glow grows around the point. Pulse begins.
- A single thin connecting line draws itself slowly to a neighbor.

### 3. LIGHT BREAKS (Prism Dispersion)
- The single line expands into a soft optical refraction. NOT a decorative rainbow or vector lines.
- It is an atmospheric light phenomenon: spectral color separation, diffused glow, and feathered edges.
- Colors from the seasonal palette blend with additive/screen opacity to feel like projected light in the void.
- The effect emerges gradually and delicately from the darkness.
- Warm, neutral, and cool tones layer irregularly to create a realistic chromatic dispersion.

### 4. MEANING GROWS (Organic branches)
- From each colored ray, organic fern/root-like branches begin to grow.
- Each branch inherits the COLOR of its parent ray.
- The warm ray grows warm-tinted branches. The cool ray grows cool-tinted branches.
- Growth is slow, recursive, asymmetric, poetic.
- NOT a literal plant. A cosmic organic drawing that emerges from split light.

### 5. TEXT REVEALED
- Only after growth has begun, a short poetic text fades in near the point.
- The text is open, non-predictive, language of possibility and horizon.

## Seasonal Color Palettes
- Spring: soft green-blue, blooming light
- Summer: warm gold, deep blue
- Autumn: purple, amber, low light
- Winter: dark blue, silver, cold quiet

## What NOT to Do
- No zodiac icons or symbols
- No immediate full map on load
- No literal plant illustrations
- No game UI or busy dashboard
- No horoscope design language
